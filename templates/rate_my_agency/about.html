About Rate my Agency!

Our aim is to help tenants gain knowledge on letting agencies, so that they may make an informed decision when it's time to move.


This is done through a comment box which users may contribute to, leaving their opinion on that particular agency, or a rating, for which they may leave a like or dislike


Agencies can also see these comments and ratings, so they may receive it as feedback for their service.



We hope you enjoy using our website.
